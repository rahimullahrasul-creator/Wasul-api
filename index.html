How It Works

Get your address in three steps

No paperwork, no waiting. Register your home location once and use your Wasul code everywhere you order.

1

Pin Your Home

Open the registration page and drop a pin on your home's exact location using GPS or the interactive map.

2

Get Your Code

Instantly receive a unique Wasul code tied to your location. Use it like a street address whenever you order.

3

Receive Deliveries

Share your code with restaurants, shops, or any delivery service. Drivers navigate straight to your door.
